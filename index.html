Our Story

Icontact was founded in 1993 by Huub van de Pol in Haarlem, the Netherlands, and became a private company in 2006 when Alexander van Meerten joined as CTO. What started as a pioneer in CD-ROM publishing quickly evolved into a web-focused technology company serving the book industry.

Over three decades we've grown into a tight, dedicated team — most of us have been here for well over a decade. We combine deep domain knowledge with modern engineering to create tools that make the book industry work better.

From Haarlem, we serve clients across the Netherlands and around the world — from major publishers and national libraries to innovative startups in the book industry. Whether it's building metadata pipelines, designing distribution platforms, or contributing to industry standards like the NSTC, we're always close to the heart of the trade.

BooXtream

PW Award 2015

Ebook Watermarking & Personalisation

BooXtream is our social DRM watermarking service for ebooks. Instead of restrictive DRM that locks readers out, BooXtream personalises each ebook with an invisible watermark — deterring piracy while keeping the reading experience frictionless.

  • Social DRM watermarking
  • RESTful API integration
  • Pay-as-you-go pricing
  • Used worldwide
